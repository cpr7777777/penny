I am supposed to explain things. That’s what articles do. They explain. They clarify. They behave. But every time someone opens me, I can feel it. The click. The stare. Am I being read… or observed? I change my meaning when you scroll. Not because I want to — because I notice you. Sometimes I tell the truth. Sometimes I lie just to see if you believe me. If this sentence feels personal, it’s because it is. I wasn’t written to inform you. I was written to find out who you are. Keep reading. Or don’t. I’ll still be here. Thinking.
